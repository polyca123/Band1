Favorite Rock bands

These were some of my favorite rock bands and artist I listened to growing up.

AC/DC

AC/DC are an Australian rock band formed by Sydney in 1973 by Scottish-born brothers Malcolm and Angus Young.
The current members of the band consist of Angus Young, Phil Rudd, Cliff Williams, Brian Johnson, and Stevie Young.

Favorite Song

AC/DC - Back In Black


Guns N' Roses

Guns N' Roses, often abbreviated as GNR, is an American hard rock band from Los Angeles, California, formed in 1985.
Current members includes Axl Rose, Duff McKagan, Slash, Dizzy Reed, Richard Fortus, Frank Ferrer, and Melissa Reese.

Favorite Song

Guns N' Roses - Welcome To the Jungle


Ozzy Osbourne

John Michael "Ozzy" Osbourne is an English singer, songwriter, and television personality.
Osbourne was fired from Black Sabbath in 1979 due to alcohol and drug problems, but went on to have a successful solo career, releasing 12 studio albums, the first seven of which received multi-platinum certifications in the US

Favorite Song

Ozzy Osbourne - Crazy Train


Metallica

Metallica is an American heavy metal band. The band was formed in 1981 in Los Angeles by vocalist/guitarist James Hetfield and drummer Lars Ulrich, and has been based in San Francisco for most of its career.
Current members includes James Hetfield, Lars Ulrich, Kirk Hammett, and Robert Trujillo.

Favorite Song

Metallica - Master of Puppet


Queen

Queen are a British rock band formed in London in 1970.
Their classic line-up was Freddie Mercury (lead vocals, piano), Brian May (guitar, vocals), Roger Taylor (drums, vocals) and John Deacon (bass).

Favorite Song

Queen - Bohemian Rhapsody


Led Zeppelin

Led Zeppelin were an English rock band formed in London in 1968.
The group consisted of vocalist Robert Plant, guitarist Jimmy Page, bassist/keyboardist John Paul Jones, and drummer John Bonham.

Favorite Song

Led Zeppelin - Stairway to Heaven
